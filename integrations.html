Cludo Search

A hosted inter and intranet indexing and searching solution. A prior client desired that this product be used for indexing and searching of a then-existing Obtree CMS intranet and internet site. The integration involved some client-side widget embedding, as well as some more involved server-side C# integration to enable remote indexing and subsequent querying of the remotely stored data.
